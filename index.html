About

Alignment before execution

Working Method Flexible

Regional Expertise DACH

Hebel is a strategic advisory firm supporting companies in building the right partnerships, securing suitable funding, and structuring high-value collaborations.

We work with technology-driven businesses, particularly in electronics, data, and innovation-heavy sectors, helping them translate internal capabilities into concrete commercial and strategic opportunities.

Our work is centered around the DACH region, while supporting cross-border collaborations where strategic value is created.

Engagements are structured based on project complexity and objectives, ranging from focused strategic phases to multi-month execution support.

Use Cases

You’re a diversified industrial company ready to launch something new

You build complex products, have strong production capabilities, and even some free capacity — but now you want to introduce a new, innovative product into the market through the right partners and channels.

We help you identify high-fit partners, design collaboration models, and make targeted introductions that turn capacity and innovation into real commercial traction.

You’ve launched a new product — now it needs real-world adoption (and funding)

You’re an early-stage company with a fresh, innovative solution, but getting it into real customer environments often requires pilots, integration partners, and funded projects that reduce risk for early adopters.

We help you structure pilot use cases, connect you with MSPs and strategic partners, and secure suitable funding to accelerate adoption and market validation.

You have a proven product — now you want to win more end customers

Your technology is established and reliable, but complex customer projects rarely close without integrators, MSPs, and solution partners delivering the full stack around it.

We help you build the right partner ecosystem, align roles and incentives, structure joint offerings, and orchestrate collaborations that turn opportunities into closed customer deals.

You have strong capabilities — and need funding to turn them into real opportunities

You know what your company can deliver, but transforming expertise into concrete market projects often depends on clear structure and the right financing.

We help you shape investment-ready initiatives, connect capabilities to real customer needs, and secure public or private funding to turn potential into execution.
